The Mood Mosaic Vol. 13

The Mood Mosaic series is a famous Vinyl compilation with Funk, Rare Groove and Disco tracks ('60s-'80s).

I recently got the Vol. 13 and I really enjoyed the tracks Closer Closer (Celi Bee & The Fuzzy Bunch) and Midnight Groove (Love Unlimited) among others. For the latter, there is a less known re-edit back from 2005. It can be found on Juno and also on Discogs marketplace.

That is the turntable I have (Technics 1210 M5G).

Enjoy.
