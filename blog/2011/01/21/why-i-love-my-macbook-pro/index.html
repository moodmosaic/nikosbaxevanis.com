Why I Love My MacBook Pro

by Nikos Baxevanis in miscellaneous

It is the perfect solution if you want to work out, for example at Starbucks. I take a long break every time I need to charge the battery. This means that every ~4-6* hours I will get a an hour off for full refreshment. :-)

* with Visual Studio, VirtualBox and about 25-30 open tabs in Chrome.
